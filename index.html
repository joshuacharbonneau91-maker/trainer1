About the progression
Each phase unlocks after completing a set number of sessions — go at your own pace. Regular and reverse kegel sessions both count toward phase progression. A pulse finisher (10 × 1s fast contractions) is added from Phase 2 onward to train fast-twitch fibers alongside the endurance work.
